My First Week At AkiraChix

Experience

My first week at AkiraChix was all about adjusting to the new program, everyday being a learning opportunity. I loved how the staff was very welcoming and ready to support us to make it easier for us to adapt.

Good Things I have Experienced

  1. I have interacted with people from diverse nationalities and communities
  2. The environment is serene for learning
  3. The alumnis have been resourceful in academics

The Not So Good Experiences

  • Confusion and an unclear program especially during the weekend
  • The place is very cold especially in the morning
